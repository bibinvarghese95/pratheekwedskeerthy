Our Story

How it all began

Where It All Began
Two Hearts, One Hospital

Fate had its own plans when Pratheek and Keerthy both found themselves working at the same hospital. What started as familiar faces in the corridors soon turned into lingering conversations, shared coffee breaks, and a connection neither of them could ignore.

A New Chapter in the UK
Miles Apart, Hearts Together

When Pratheek moved to the United Kingdom, the distance only deepened what they already felt. Late-night video calls across time zones, handwritten letters, and the quiet ache of missing someone — until the day Keerthy made the leap and joined him in the UK.

Love in Every Season
Falling in Love, All Over Again

Together in a new country, they built a world of their own — weekend adventures through the Scottish Highlands, cosy evenings with home-cooked Kerala meals, and a love that grew stronger with every shared sunrise. Every moment felt like coming home.

November 25, 2025
The Proposal — Her Birthday Surprise

On Keerthy's birthday, Pratheek had planned the most magical surprise. Surrounded by fairy lights and the warm glow of a Christmas tree, he got down on one knee and asked her to be his forever. With tears of joy and a heart overflowing with love — she said YES!

September 5, 2026
Forever Begins

From a hospital corridor in Kerala to the rolling hills of the UK, their journey has been nothing short of extraordinary. And now, surrounded by the blessings of family and friends, they begin the most beautiful chapter of all. We can't wait to celebrate with you!
